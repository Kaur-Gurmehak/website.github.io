Our Story

El Cielo - Heaven On Earth



Welcome to El Cielo - a 2 Michelin Star fine dining restaurant, where we're serving the most robust flavors every season. El Cielo brings incredible blends of Italian culture to India. The restaurant offers fine dining with a blended cultural theme of Modern Italy and India. El Cielo has been offering heart into the plates since 1986. The authentic Italian delicacies, cooked fresh, worked up the popularity. Renowned for our deliciously-unique farm-to-table dining experience, El Cielo's prides ourselves on our refreshingly bold rendition of Italian modern cuisine. With our 25+ years of excellence, we are proud winners of 16 national and international awards including the prestigious James Beard Award by James Beard Foundation, USA.
